Famous allows users to challenge their friends in talent competitions within categories such as Music, Sports, Beauty, Comedy and more. The more likes users get from their fans, the higher they reach the top ranks to claim fame and win prizes.

Where is Famous today?

Today Famous users are creating videos and posting them to challenges and winning REAL PRIZES. Many people are sharing their Famous videos on other social networks such as Twitter, Facebook and Instagram getting more exposure and watching friends and other Famous users around the world taking on the challenges.
